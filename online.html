——Carter Family Fan-Site

Framed as a fan page for the country music group The Carter Family, this website was designed as part of a larger project about fictionalised country-music histories, obsessive fandom and the inernet. The content is almost entirely fictional, and the framing device of the Carter Family is used as a backdrop to create a series of short, throwaway musical compositions that I continue to add to.
