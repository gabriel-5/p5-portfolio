Online Projects

——Please Call

Basic idea for the frontend of a new social media site intended to sit somewhere between a communal Twitter feed and lonely hearts listings. Anyone lonely can post their name, message and a phone number for strangers to call them up. Likely fundamentally unusable but potentially interesting outcomes.

——Skid Tiller

Rough prototype for a planned multimedia text-adventure being developed in collaboration with a writer/artist. I will be designing the mechanics, composing the soundtrack and doing a portion of the writing while my collaborator will cover the bulk of the narrative - which will be themed around body horror and experiences of gender - as they begin an artist residency October 2021. This was a quick build created as a tone-setting exercise.

— — —>

——Carter Family Fan-Site

Framed as a fan page for the country music group The Carter Family and visually modelled after historic video game fanpages, this website was designed as part of a larger project about fictionalised country-music histories, obsessive fandom and the internet. The content is almost entirely fictional, and the framing device of the Carter Family is used as a backdrop to create a series of short, throwaway musical compositions that I will continue to add to as an ongoing project.

— — —>

——King's Field Personality Test

Multiple-choice quiz website which tells the user which NPC from the video game King's Field (1994) is their "personality type". The page is supposed to be aesthetically reminiscent of old flash games, and I also based the sound design in memories of playing these.

— — —>

——Churchy

Short video game made in the Sword of Moonlight engine. The game was created with the intent of directing music video inside it, with the characters in the game speaking the lyrics to the song.
