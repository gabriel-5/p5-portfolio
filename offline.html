——Circuit-Bent Table Installation (2018)

DIY musical instrument designed for improvisation and collaboration. Table made from found wood and metal, and the insides of old keyboards. The keyboard parts were circuit-bent and attached to the metal objects on top of the table. Participants could make sound by connecting themselves to two metal objects (or to other participants), completing the circuit. The intention was to create a new instrument that anybody could play and improvise with, especially those with no background in music.
